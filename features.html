Platform Modules

1. Data Ingestion Engine

Our multi-modal ingestion system handles unstructured and structured financial data.

  • Messy Indian PDFs: Layout-aware OCR and text extraction from scanned Annual Reports, sanction letters, and legal notices.
  • GST Extraction: Automated parsing of GSTR-3B / GSTR-1, with GSTR-2A vs 3B reconciliation flags.
  • Bank Normalization: Standardizing transaction descriptions across different bank formats.
  • Legal Scan: Identifying key clauses in legal notices and contracts.
  • Forensic Cross-Checks: GST ↔ bank triangulation to surface circular trading and revenue inflation signals.
  • Databricks Lakehouse: Delta ingestion layer for audit-grade lineage, replay, and analytics at scale.

2. Secondary Research Agent

An autonomous agent that scans the web for external risks and intelligence.

  • Promoter Checks: Background verification with MCA filings (directorships, charges, and compliance signals).
  • Litigation Flags: Litigation history from the e-Courts portal / NCLT orders, plus news scanning.
  • Media Risk: Sentiment analysis of news articles related to the entity.
  • Sector Intelligence: Sector-specific headwinds (including RBI regulations/circulars) affecting the borrower.
  • Primary Insights: Credit officer site-visit notes captured as qualitative inputs for the CAM.

3. Explainable AI Engine

Transparent scoring logic based on the fundamental Five Cs of Credit.

  • Five Cs Model: Character, Capacity, Capital, Collateral, and Conditions.
  • Risk Grading: Dynamic scoring (0-100) mapped to internal rating grades (A to B).
  • Officer Override: Allows credit officers to apply judgment adjustments (+/- score).

4. CAM Generator

Produces a standardized, committee-ready Credit Appraisal Memo.

  • Structured Output: Clean HTML/PDF format ready for presentation.
  • Committee-Ready: Includes executive summary, financial snapshot, and recommendation.
  • Export Architecture: One-click download for offline archiving.
