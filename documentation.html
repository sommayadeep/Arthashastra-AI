Introduction to Mauryan Intelligence

Arthashastra AI is an institutional-grade Credit Intelligence engine designed for the Indian banking sector. It synchronizes ancient principles of financial statecraft with modern explainable AI to automate complex Credit Appraisal Memorandums (CAM).

Our methodology focuses on the "Five Cs of Credit" — Character, Capacity, Capital, Collateral, and Conditions — processed through a civilizational lens of risk management.

Risk Scoring Architecture

The core of Arthashastra AI is a deterministic scoring engine that evaluates financial coefficients derived from Annual Reports, GST returns, and Bank Statements.

// Baseline Institutional Calculation
Score = (DSCR * 0.4) + (Leverage * 0.3) + (Condition Adj. * 0.3)

The engine applies "Strategic Adjustments" which allow human credit officers to inject civilizational context into the algorithmic output, ensuring AI acts as a tool, not a replacement for judgment.

Dharma Ledger Integration

The Dharma Ledger serves as your cryptographically secured vault of institutional history. Every assessment generated is recorded with a unique Mauryan Grade (A+, BBB, etc.) and archived for audit-ready compliance.

Administrators can use the Intelligence HUD to filter through decades of historical credit decisions, identifying patterns in sector-specific defaults or growth trajectories.

AI Underwriting Meta

Our AI operates on an Explainable AI (XAI) framework. You can inspect the exact metrics and reasoning the model used to arrive at a risk grade.

Unlike black-box models, Arthashastra AI provides an immutable audit trail, pinpointing exactly how Days Past Due (DPD) or high utilization ratios influenced the final Credit Appraisal Memorandum.

Security & Sovereignty

We believe in absolute data sovereignty. Data operates completely client-side via your browser; Arthashastra AI does not harvest your institutional financial data to external servers or train overarching public models.

All stored case files are encrypted in your local Dharma Ledger (browser storage), guaranteeing that your credit evaluations never leak beyond your local environment.
